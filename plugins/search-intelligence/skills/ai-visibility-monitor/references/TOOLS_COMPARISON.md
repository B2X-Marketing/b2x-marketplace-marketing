## Detailed Profiles

### Profound

**Focus**: Enterprise-grade AI visibility analytics.

**Key differentiators**:
- SOC 2 compliance — required for enterprise procurement
- GA4 attribution — connects AI visibility to actual traffic and conversions
- Multi-region and multilingual — tracks AI responses in different languages and geographies
- Covers ChatGPT, Gemini, Claude, Perplexity, and Copilot
- Custom reporting and API access

**Pricing**: $500-1,000+/mo depending on brand count, region count, and feature tier.

**Best for**: Enterprise brands with compliance requirements, multi-region presence, and need for deep analytics integration with existing marketing technology stack.

**Limitation**: Overkill for small operations. Minimum commitment typically required.

---

### Otterly AI

**Focus**: Lightweight brand visibility tracking across AI platforms.

**Key differentiators**:
- Simple setup — get started in minutes
- 10,000+ active users — proven product
- Tracks brand mentions inside AI-generated responses
- Basic competitor comparison
- Affordable entry point

**Pricing**: $50-200/mo depending on brand count and prompt volume.

**Best for**: SMBs and solo marketers wanting quick, affordable monitoring without complex configuration.

**Limitation**: Less depth than enterprise tools. Limited customization and reporting options.

---

### Peec AI

**Focus**: Real-time AI visibility reporting with geographic breakdown.

**Key differentiators**:
- Real-time monitoring and alerts
- Region-specific tracking — see how AI describes your brand in different markets
- AI assistant breakdown — per-platform granularity
- Multi-user support for teams
- Competitor tracking included

**Pricing**: $50-150/mo depending on features and user count.

**Best for**: Teams needing real-time insights, especially those operating in multiple regions or languages.

**Limitation**: Newer entrant — smaller user base than Otterly AI.

---

### AIclicks

**Focus**: Citation tracking and click attribution from AI platforms.

**Key differentiators**:
- Specialized in citation-to-click attribution
- Tracks which AI citations drive actual traffic
- Cross-platform citation analysis
- Source quality scoring

**Pricing**: Contact for pricing (varies by volume).

**Best for**: Brands focused on measuring the traffic impact of AI citations, not just visibility.

**Limitation**: Narrower focus — less comprehensive brand sentiment and narrative analysis.

---

### SE Ranking Visible

**Focus**: GEO monitoring integrated with traditional SEO toolkit.

**Key differentiators**:
- Tracks AI search results across 8 platforms
- Content optimization scoring for AI readiness
- Keyword-to-prompt mapping — bridges SEO and AEO
- Integrated with SE Ranking's full SEO suite

**Pricing**: Bundled with SE Ranking plans (varies).

**Best for**: SEO teams already using SE Ranking who want to add AI visibility to their workflow without a separate tool.

**Limitation**: Tied to SE Ranking ecosystem. Less standalone value if not already an SE Ranking user.

---

### Ahrefs Brand Radar

**Focus**: Brand mention monitoring with AI platform coverage.

**Key differentiators**:
- Integrated with Ahrefs' domain authority and backlink data
- Combines traditional SEO metrics with AI brand monitoring
- Leverages Ahrefs' massive web index for source analysis
- Domain Rating context for citation likelihood assessment

**Pricing**: Bundled with Ahrefs plans (varies).

**Best for**: Teams using Ahrefs for SEO who want unified brand monitoring across traditional and AI search.

**Limitation**: Tied to Ahrefs ecosystem. AI-specific features may be less mature than dedicated AI monitoring tools.

---

### Writesonic AI Visibility

**Focus**: Content optimization for AI citation readiness.

**Key differentiators**:
- Citation readiness scoring — evaluates how likely content is to be cited
- AI visibility audit — assesses current brand presence
- Content optimization recommendations — actionable fixes
- Integrates with Writesonic's content generation tools

**Pricing**: Bundled with Writesonic plans (varies).

**Best for**: Content teams wanting to combine AI visibility auditing with content optimization and generation.

**Limitation**: More content-focused than monitoring-focused. Less depth on competitive intelligence and trend tracking.

## Recommendation Framework

**Use this skill when**:
- Monitoring 1-2 brands at monthly or quarterly cadence
- Budget is a constraint ($4-10/run vs $50-1,000+/mo)
- You need D1-D3 root cause analysis alongside D4 measurement
- You want full control over the prompt library
- The brand is in early AEO/GEO adoption and needs a diagnostic, not just a dashboard

**Graduate to a market tool when**:
- Tracking 3+ brands simultaneously
- Need real-time alerts for citation changes
- Require multi-region or multilingual tracking
- Enterprise compliance requirements (SOC 2)
- Need GA4 attribution to connect AI visibility to conversions
- Team of 3+ people needs shared dashboard access
- Weekly or daily monitoring cadence required
