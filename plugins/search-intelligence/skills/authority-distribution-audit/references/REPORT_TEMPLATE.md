## Template Usage Notes

- Write the Executive Summary LAST after all sections are complete.
- Omit Section 5 (Competitor Comparison) entirely if no competitors were provided.
- Keep recommendations specific and actionable. Assign each to a responsible team.
- Lead every section with the score and key finding before diving into details.
- Use the Platform Score Summary table in Section 3 as a quick-reference dashboard.
- All statistics cited in Section 7 come from the Knowledge Base extract — do not fabricate stats.
