## What a Complete Strategy Would Add

This snapshot covers your **Purpose**, **Customer**, and **Messaging** — the essential foundation. But a complete marketing strategy also includes five additional layers that turn this foundation into an executable plan:

**Strategic Planning** — Where you stand today, where you want to be in 12 months, and the SMART objectives to get there. Includes competitive analysis and a 90-day action plan.

**Customer Journey Mapping** — How customers discover you (Reach), engage with you (Act), buy from you (Convert), and stay with you (Engage). With specific tactics and conversion targets at each stage.

**Channel & Media Strategy** — Which paid, earned, shared, and owned media channels to invest in, how much to allocate to each, and what ROI to expect.

**Content Plan** — What content to create, in what format, on which channels, and when. A 90-day editorial calendar mapped to your customer journey.

**Strategy Validation** — Cross-checking everything for coherence, identifying gaps, and stress-testing your assumptions.
